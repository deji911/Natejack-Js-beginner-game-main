How To Play

Test your luck against the Bot in this Simple yet Fun Natejack Game 2

Draw cards and try not to get the sum total of your cards above 21.
Try to Win all the Bot's Money

  • If the sum total of your cards is greater than the sum total of the bot's cards You win the Pot
    i.e Your sum = 19 & Bot's sum = 18
  • If you get a total sum of 21 and the Bot gets higher or lower than you, You win the Pot
    i.e Your sum = 21 & Bot's sum = 22
    or
    Your sum = 21 & Bot's sum = 20
  • If the first two cards drawn are Aces you automatically win regardless of the bots sum being higher than yours

* Note: If reverse is the case, The bot wins the Pot *

TIE

There are two different scenerios for a tie to occur

If you get the same total as the Bot You tie

If both you and the Bot gets above 21, You Tie

TIE BREAKER

When You and the Bot Ties a particular round, the winner of the Pot will be decided through a Tiebreaker: a minigame where u each draw a single card and whoever gets an Ace or a Higher card wins the Pot.

How to play II

You and the Bot are both giving $1000

Place bets into the Pot before drawing your cards

The Pot is where bet for that particular round are kept, then given out to the winner of said round;

  • Draws Two random cards for You and the Bot
  • This is for drawing a new card for just you..(The Bot can decide wether it wants to Hit or Stand)
  • This automatically reveals the Bot's cards and decide the winner of the Pot. When you stand: you are not drawing a new card but putting your cards on the table(virtual)

*Standing dosen't mean the Bot will..the Bot might decide to draw a new card before Standing*


Need to Know

When you draw a K,Q and J: Each carry the same total of 10
